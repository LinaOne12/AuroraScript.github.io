Frequently Asked Questions

1. Q: My game is a cracked version and Rampage
wont work, what can I do?


A: Buy the game, we do NOT support game pirating.

2. Q: What are the panel things in the top right corner
and how can I disable them?


A: They are window activators, press "x" to enable your mouse (by default)
and use your mouse to click on them. They give you information
about Rampage and your game. You can disable them by going to
Settings -> Extended Ui -> Disable Window Activators.


3. Q: How do I disable the open prompt button?


A: Go to Settings -> Extended Ui -> Disable Open Info.


4. Q: Failed to save settings? How do I fix this?


A: Go to your RDR2 or GTA V game directory and hover over "RampageFiles"
and right click on properties and then go to security and then right below
"Group or user names" where it says "To change permissions, click Edit"
click on that and then go down and click on "Full control on the
"Allow" option and click "Apply" and "Ok" and then exit and your done.
