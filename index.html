A single player mod menu / trainer developed
by RampageDev that has been around for a couple of years. Rampage has many
useful and special features.

Why Choose Rampage?

1

Tons of features

Rampage has tons of features and unique options that you can use and have fun with, you can never go wrong with Rampage.

2

Stable from the core

Rampage runs very smoothly without crashes and even giving some performance boost.

3

Fast to update

Rampage is usually pretty quick to update to a game patch as well as crashes.

4

Free

And of course the best part of it is that Rampage is free, I mean who doesn't 't like free stuff.

Made with love

Rampage is a menu for new users to modding to experience what it's like. We sometimes even take suggestions from our users to put into our menu.

Community

Rampage is a ever growing community menu that likes to please it's users and make them happy. We are always looking to improve our menu and make a better impact.
