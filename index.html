Description

Avoid the obstacles and survive as long as possible!

Color Dash is an exciting game where you control a player and navigate through obstacles of different colors. The goal is to avoid colliding with the obstacles by moving left or right using the arrow keys. The player color and obstacle color can be customized in the settings. Your score increases as you survive longer, and the game becomes more challenging based on the selected difficulty level. Enable the "Auto start after death" option to automatically restart the game after a collision. Have fun playing Color Dash!
